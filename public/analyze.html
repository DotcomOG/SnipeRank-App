SnipeRank Scoring Methodology

Our proprietary model evaluates AI Access Readiness, Trust & Verification, LLM Interpretability & Clarity, and Prompt-Pattern Alignment. Underlying heuristics include:

  • Semantic Obstruction Index — detects unclear domain terms and tangled headings.
  • Narrative Fit — checks if sections ladder to a single core claim.
  • Bot Clarity Ratio — favors structured elements (lists/tables/quotes) over dense text.
  • Disambiguation Layering — encourages concise clarifiers for entities (dates, roles, locations).

Exact weights are proprietary. Scores include a confidence band to reflect signal variance.
